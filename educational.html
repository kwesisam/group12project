Educational Background

Bachelor's degree: Quaynor received his Bachelor of Science degree in Engineering (Honors) from Dartmouth College in the United States in 1972.

Master's degree: He then earned a Master of Science degree in Computer Science from the State University of New York at Stony Brook in 1974.

Doctoral degree: Quaynor completed his Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degree in Computer Science at the University of Pennsylvania in 1977. During his studies, Quaynor focused on areas such as computer networking and distributed systems, which later became his areas of expertise.

He also conducted research on topics such as packet switching, internetworking, and computer protocols. His education and research laid the foundation for his groundbreaking work in internet development and technology entrepreneurship in Africa.
