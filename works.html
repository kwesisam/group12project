Establishment of Network Computer Systems (NCS): In the early 1990s, Quaynor founded NCS, the first internet service provider in Africa. The company played a significant role in promoting internet access and usage on the continent, and Quaynor's work with NCS helped to establish the foundation for the growth of the internet in Africa. African Network Operators Group (AfNOG): Quaynor was one of the founders of AfNOG, an organization that provides training and support to network engineers in Africa. The group has played a critical role in building and strengthening the technical infrastructure needed for the growth of the internet on the continent. Ghana Internet Service Providers Association (GISPA): Quaynor also founded GISPA, an industry association that represents internet service providers in Ghana. The organization has been instrumental in promoting the growth of the internet in Ghana and has helped to create a more favorable regulatory environment for the industry. Development of local language tools: Quaynor has been involved in efforts to develop software and tools that support the use of local languages in digital communication. He has advocated for the use of African languages on the internet, and his work has helped to create tools that allow for the use of local languages in email, instant messaging, and other digital communications. Cybersecurity: Quaynor has been a leading advocate for cybersecurity in Africa and has worked to raise awareness of the importance of protecting digital information. He has contributed to several papers and articles on cybersecurity issues in Africa and has been involved in developing policies and best practices for protecting digital assets on the continent. Healthcare technology: Quaynor has also been involved in efforts to use technology to improve healthcare outcomes in Africa. He has advocated for the use of telemedicine and other digital technologies to improve access to healthcare services in rural areas, and he has worked with organizations to develop tools and systems that support these efforts.
