father of internet in Africa

PROF NII QUAYNOR

Dr. Nii Quaynor is considered the father of the internet in Africa because of his pioneering workin establishing and promoting the use of the internet in the continent. Here are some of his notable achievements

Establishing the first internet connection in Africa: In 1992, Dr. Quaynor established the first internet connection in Africa, which was a major milestone in the development of the internet on the continent. This paved the way for further internet connectivity and development in Africa.

Founding the first internet service provider in Ghana: Dr. Quaynor founded the first internet service provider (ISP) in Ghana, called Network Computer Systems (NCS), which provided internet connectivity to individuals and organizations in the country. This was a crucial step in the development of the internet in Ghana and in Africa as a whole.

Promoting internet development and awareness: Dr. Quaynor has been a strong advocate for the development and use of the internet in Africa. He has worked tirelessly to promote internet development, awareness, and accessibility in the continent, including advocating for policies and regulations that support the growth of the internet in Africa.

Overall, Dr.Quaynor's contributions have been crucial in advancing the development of the internet in Africa, and he is widely recognized as a pioneer and a leader in the field of internet development in the continent.
